About

Made In Mississippi

"Come home when the streetlights come on," my mom used to say to me. Dirt under my nails and fresh off hours of imagination, I finally did. Barefoot adventures in the Mississippi Delta branded me: disciplined, instinctive, bold. But small-town dreams need sculpting. So after high school, I moved to Savannah, Georgia where I earned my degree at the Savannah College of Art and Design in 2007. After that, I did a brief stint in New York, but the Big Apple proved to be just a little to big for this southern chick.

Today, I'm living in Louisville, Kentucky and working as a UX designer at Humana. With 12 years of professional design experience, I have a keen eye for both digital and print; my background includes UX/UI, branding, layout, art direction, and app and website design. Over the years, I have collaborated with some pretty awesome humans including developers, marketers, copywriters, clients, photographers, and stylists.
