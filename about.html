About

Made In Mississippi

“Come home when the streetlights come on,” my mom used to say to me. Dirt under my nails and fresh off hours of imagination, I did. Barefoot adventures in Rolling Fork, Mississippi branded me: disciplined, instinctive, bold. But small-town dreams need sculpting. After high school, I moved to Savannah, Georgia where I finished my degree at the prestigious Savannah College of Art and Design.

Today, I’m an award-winning designer with more than a decade of experience in digital, print, logo, and layout design. I’m the Art Director at DBS Interactive, a full-service digital agency in Louisville, KY. Every client is unique, and every day requires a different design solution. The quality of that work never wavers and neither does my aim — outstanding and hardworking user-centric design.
